Egg and Cheese Sandwich

Description

What is there to say? A classic, grilled, succulant egg and cheese sandwich for your breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 slice of American cheese

Steps

Egg

  1. Crack the egg into a microwave-safe cereal bowl and whisk in the milk.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook in the microwave on 100% power for 1 to 2 minutes, or until cooked through

Sandwich Assembly

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Use a spoon to remove the cooked egg from the bowl and set it on one piece of toast.
  3. Top with a slice of cheese and the other piece of toast.
  4. Cook in the microwave until cheese is melted, about 15 seconds.
