Bang Bang Sauce

Description

Tangy, sweet, spicy, and most importantly delicious. A nice coating for those quick snacks and small finger foods, it'll make sure to make your breaks, car rides, and even lunch time a delight.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sweet Thai chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Stir together mayonnaise, Sriracha, chili sauce, and rice vinegar in a small ball until the sauce is smooth
